Effective Date: January 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FJAP Industries collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Resizely app.

1. Information We Access

Resizely requires access to files and media on your device to allow PDF editing and manipulation. The following information may be accessed:

  • Files stored locally on your device, including PDFs and images you choose to open in the app
  • File metadata (such as file name, size, and type) required for editing and saving

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide PDF editing functionality and save changes to your device
  • To improve app features, stability, and performance (only anonymized metadata, never content)
  • To respond to support requests if you contact us

3. Data Storage

Resizely does not upload or share your files with any servers or third parties. All file access and edits remain strictly on your device unless you explicitly share them yourself.

4. Third-Party Services
